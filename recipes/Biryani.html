Biryani

Description

Biryani is an intricate rice dish made with layers of curried meat and rice. Given its use of adornments and luxurious finishes, it’s no surprise that biryani has roots in Persian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Whole Spices
  • Marinated Chicken
  • Oil/Ghee
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger & Garlic
  • Dried Plums

Steps

  1. Prepare the biryani masala (or use store-bought) and marinate the chicken.
  2. Cook the chicken curry. While it’s cooking over low heat, prepare the rice.
  3. Bring a pot of water to a boil and parboil the rice. Drain and set aside.
  4. Bring it all together for a final steam (‘dum‘).
